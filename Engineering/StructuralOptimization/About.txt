//Sources
Bracken, J, and McCormick, G P, Chapter 6. In Selected Applications of Nonlinear Programming. John Wiley and Sons, New York, 1968, pp. 50-57.

//Type
NLP

//Description
Design of a vertically corrugated transverse bulkhead of an oil tanker. The model determines
the design that minimizes the total weight of the transverse bulkhead subject to constraints
on performance characteristics and on certain dimensions.
