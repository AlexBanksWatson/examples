CapacitatedWarehouseLocation

//Sources
J.E.Beasley "An algorithm for solving large capacitated warehouse location problems" European Journal of Operational Research 33 (1988) 314-325.

//Type
MIP

//Description
The capacitated warehouse location problem is the 
problem of locating a number of warehouses which 
have to service a set of customers, at minimum cost, 
where each customer has an associated demand and 
there are constraints on the total demand that can 
be met from a warehouse.
